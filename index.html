What is Pwnadelphia?

Pwnadelphia is a fictional city built to be vulnerable on purpose. Think: a Wild West version of Philly where every bar, bank, and back alley is a lesson in bad security choices.

The goal isn't crime. The goal is education: learn to find bugs, exploit them in a safe sandbox, and understand how to fix them in the real world.
